About Imex Central

The Imex Central Site provide access to the databases used for coordinating curation of the interaction data by the members of the IMEx Consortium. The participating databases share the curation load by collecting data from non-overlapping sources, processing it according to common curation rules and sharing the curated interaction data.

Databases participating in the coordinated data curation include:

  • DIP
  • IntAct
  • MINT
  • MPact
  • BioGRID

About Imex Central

The Imex Central site is used by the IMEx databases to cordinate curation of the interaction data by the members of the IMEx Consortium. The participating databases share the curation load by collecting data from non-overlapping sets of publications and process them according to common set of curation rules

Databases participating in the coordinated data curation include, as of Nov 11th, 2012:

  • DIP
  • IntAct
  • MatrixDB
  • MINT

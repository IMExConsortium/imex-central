Welcome to ImexCentral

IMEx Central is designed to coordinate curation efforts between the IMEx partners - a group of interaction databases that agreed to conform to common curation and data exchange formats in order to improve curation efficiency.

Databases actively participating in the coordinated data curation include:

DIP IntAct MINT MPact BioGRID
